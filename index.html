Types of Economics

  • 1.Microeconomics
  • 2.Macroeconomics

    • 1. microeconomics which deals with particular variable in small scale eg income of family,profit of firm etc..

    2. macroeconomics which deals with large scale variable particulary nation's income,employment,growth rate,gpd,gnp etc

    3.Monetary economics

    3.Monetary economics deals with controling money supply to stablise the economy from inflation central bank does that job

    Monetary policy instrument

    Repo Rate

    The rate at which commerical banks(sbi,indian bank,hdfc..etc)borrows money from central bank(Reserve bank of india)

    Reverse repo Rate

    The rate at which central bank(RBI)borrows money from commerical banks for reducing the money supply
